Flood Info

Welcome to the Pu Ngaol Flood website, we aim to provide you some helpful information to help keep you and others safe from the dangers around us through the services we provide on our website.

As the climate and world continues to change, nature does and well so we have to make sure we are all ready when this happens, so, we decided to create this website to educate and everyone on what to do if a flood were to happen.

Here are some steps you could take:

1. Follow any instructions if possible from any emergency services around and stay calm
2. Immediatley get to Higher Ground - it is important to stay out of reach of fast moving water as it can easily sweep you away
3. If possible you can try to use your phone to attempt to contact emergency services if required or use our website to get the latest data on the floods.

Learn more about how flooding within Pu Ngaol works through our Simulation program as well as check out all of our other services that we hope can help you keep protected and safe from the world around us.
